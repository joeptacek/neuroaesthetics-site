Contact the Center

Penn Center for Neuroaesthetics represents a collaborative effort by individuals located around the world, but here at the University of Pennsylvania, it's primarily situated within the Neurology Department at the Perelman School of Medicine.

PCfN is also formally affiliated with the School of Arts and Sciences, School of Design, and Wharton School.
