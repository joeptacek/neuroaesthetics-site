Who we are

We bring people together whose expertise converge on neuroaesthetics scientific aims. This includes University of Pennsylvania faculty from the Perelman School of Medicine, School of Arts and Sciences, School of Design, and Wharton School, as well as other collaborating individuals and groups from around the world.
