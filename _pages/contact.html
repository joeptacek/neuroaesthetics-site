Contact the center

Penn Center for Neuroaesthetics represents a collaborative effort by individuals located around the world, but here at University of Pennsylvania it's primarily situated within the Department of Neurology at the Perelman School of Medicine.

PCfN is also formally affiliated with the School of Arts and Sciences, School of Design, and Wharton School.
